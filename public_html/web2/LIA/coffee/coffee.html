Coffee is a very popular beverage made from seeds known as coffee beans. Coffee beans can be cultivated from Southern Africa and Asia. People tend to reach for coffee, bceause of either it's taste, stimulating effects, health benefits, and social and cultural aspects. Generally, people tend to reach for coffee in the morning, because of it's caffeine effects. Coffee can be made in different ways to create Espressos, Lattes, Americanos, Mochas, Cold Brews, Cappuccinos, etc. All these coffees can be made with a coffee machine, but not all coffee machines are the same. Some machines can be easy to use and some are difficult to handle.
